* Polynomial
** Monomial
A monomial is an expression with one term
Examples:
\[
5
\]
\[
3x
\]
\[
-2x^2
\]
\[
7ab
\]

** Binomial
is an expression with two terms
Examples:
\[
x + 3
\]
\[
2x^2 - 5x
\]
\[
a + b
\]

** Polynomial
is an expression with one two or more terms
\[
x^2 + 3x + 2 \quad (\text{trinomial})
\]
\[
4x^3 - 2x + 7
\]
\[
5 \quad (\text{monomial, but still a polynomial})
\]
