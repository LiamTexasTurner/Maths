* SquaringBiNomials
[[https://www.desmos.com/calculator/dss5zcc13e][graph]]
Work through:

\[
(x + a)^2
\]

Step 1: Write as multiplication

\[
(x + a)^2 = (x + a)(x + a)
\]

Step 2: Distribute the first bracket

\[
(x + a)(x + a) = x(x + a) + a(x + a)
\]

Step 3: Multiply the \(x\) through

\[
x(x + a) = x \cdot x + x \cdot a
\]
\[
= x^2 + ax
\]

Step 4: Multiply the \(a\) through

\[
a(x + a) = a \cdot x + a \cdot a
\]
\[
= ax + a^2
\]

Step 5: Add the results

\[
(x^2 + ax) + (ax + a^2)
\]

Step 6: Combine like terms

\[
x^2 + ax + ax + a^2
\]
\[
x^2 + 2ax + a^2
\]

Final answer:

\[
(x + a)^2 = x^2 + 2ax + a^2
\]
